What ProjectBalls does

The user story ProjectBalls wanted to solve is the lack of freely available video editing softwares, designed for coaches.
ProjectBalls is a Functional, Simplistic and Elegant software aiming to provide ease for coaches in editing snippets from long gameplay videos for students.
